# AXENIOR Brand Identity Summary

Create a trendy, simple, and high-end landing page for AXENIOR, a senior AI transformation partner.
Please refer to the brand identity defined below and the axenior-brand-identity-v2.html document.

## Core Value
- AX (AI Transformation) + Senior Expertise
- Focus: Manufacturing, Quality, Legacy system modernization

## Design Language
- **Background:** Deep Space (#0A0E17)
- **Primary Color:** AX Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Gradient:** AX Blue (#3B82F6) to AX Violet (#8B5CF6)
- **Secondary:** Navy (#0F1628), Silver (#94A3B8), White (#F8F9FC)

## Typography
- Logo: Bebas Neue
- English Heading/Slogan: Sora
- Korean Body/Heading: Noto Sans KR
- Technical/Mono: JetBrains Mono

## Slogan
- AI Transformation, Senior Expertise (AI 전환의 시니어 파트너)

## Tone of Voice
- Precise, Grounded, Senior (Professional, direct, experience-based)
